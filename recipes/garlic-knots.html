Garlic Knots

Description:

Bread with garlic. Goes great with lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag pizza dough
  • 1 stick butter
  • minced garlic

Steps:

  1. Roll out dough and cut strips
  2. Knot
  3. Bake in oven until done
  4. Melt butter and minced garlic on low heat in saucepan
  5. Toss cooked dough knots in garlic butter
  6. Eeeeeat
